Bringing Joy to the Elderly

About Us

We are Grade 9 - St. Mark, a class united by our shared commitment to embodying care, compassion, and love. Guided by our 5 Marist Pillars, we have taken the initiative to extend our hearts and hands to the elderly residents of St. Pio Home for the Aged. This mission is more than just a projectâ€”it is a calling from God. We believe that every act of kindness reflects His love and brings joy to those who need it most. Through our efforts, we aim to make a difference in the lives of the elderly by providing comfort, fostering meaningful connections, and reminding them that they are cherished and valued. With faith as our foundation and service as our purpose, we strive to honor the teachings of Christ by uplifting others and spreading His light. Together, as Grade 9 - St. Mark, we embrace this opportunity to live out our mission of care, value, and love.

Our Programs

From entertaining, interacting, loving, caring, feeding, and even playing manageable activities for the elderly to offer a variety of programs to engage and uplift our elderly community.
